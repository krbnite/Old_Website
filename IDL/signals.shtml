PMCurve Returns 7 power means for p in {-Inf, -2, -1, 0, 1, 2, Inf}. Note that p=-Inf is min(data), p=Inf is max(data), p=-1 is the harmonic mean, p=0 is the geometric mean, p=1 is the arithmetic mean, and p=2 is root-mean-square. (What is p=-2?)
Probogram Returns a contour plot where the x-axis is window time, the y-axis is in the data units, and the color axis is intensity/count. In other words, what I call "probogram" is a sliding histogram.
PSD Periodogram or modified-Periodogram estimate of the Power Spectral Density.
PSumSeq Partial sum sequence: p[i] = a*(data[0]+...+data[i-1])+data[i], a is in [-1,1].
SpecFlat Determines the flatness of a data vector's power spectra by dividing the geometric mean by the arithmetic mean.
